Unemployment Initial Claim Submit Process

     

Payment Options
All unemployment Insurance payments are electronic. When an unemployment benefit payment is made, the payment is made by either a:
  • Deposit made to an unemployment debit card click here to view Fee and Other Important Disclosures; or
  • Direct deposit to a personal checking or savings account.Deposits can only be made to banks in the U.S

Your payments will be made to an unemployment debit card unless you select direct deposit and complete the information below or if there is a problem with your direct deposit information.
